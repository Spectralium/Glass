ABOUT

I live and work in Liverpool, UK.

Put simply, I have made a lot of stained glass pieces, I enjoyed the work immensely, and I'm quite chuffed with the final results, so I'd like to show you!

I have found it very easy to find inspiration for stained glass projects. Flags, with their rich colours and simple shapes, are perfect symbols to recreate with stained glass.

So I had my hallmark: flags. And with the flags were nations, with cultures, of dance, of food, and music, and sport, all providing wonderful subject matter for stained glass projects.
